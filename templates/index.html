Trends of Olympic Medal Winners

The following plots show the trends of Olympic medal winners of ages, heights and weights. They use Google Fusion Table for visualization.

Do Olympic medal winners become more and more younger?

Age vs. Year

No. Olympic medal winners become older and older.

Do the heights of Olympic medal winners change over years?

Height vs. Year

No. The heights of Olympic medal winners stay relatively unchanged.

Do the weights of Olympic medal winners change over years?

Weight vs. Year

No. The weights of Olympic medal winners stay relatively unchanged.
